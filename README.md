# E-Commerce-Customer-Analytics-Retention-Dashboard
## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer behavior, revenue trends, product performance, retention patterns, and customer lifetime value using a real-world e-commerce dataset.

The objective is to help business stakeholders identify revenue drivers, customer retention opportunities, high-value customer segments, and product performance trends through data-driven insights.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce companies often struggle to answer:

- Which customers generate the most revenue?
- Which customers are at risk of churning?
- Which product categories drive sales?
- How well are customers retained over time?
- What is the lifetime value of different customer segments?

This project addresses these questions using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

---

## Dataset

### Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset

Dataset contains:

- 113,383 Transactions
- 95,122 Orders
- 92,075 Customers
- Multiple product categories
- Payment and delivery information

## Key Business Insights

### Revenue Insights

- Revenue increased significantly from 2017 to 2018.
- São Paulo generated the highest revenue among all states.

### Product Insights

- Bed, Bath & Table products generated the highest revenue.
- Beauty & Health products ranked among the top-selling categories.

### Customer Insights

- Loyal Customers represent the largest customer segment.
- At-Risk Customers contribute substantial revenue despite declining engagement.
- VIP customers generate the majority of customer lifetime value.

### Retention Insights

- Customer retention decreases significantly after the first purchase.
- Retention-focused campaigns could improve long-term revenue.
